Andy Haryana Club
Celebrating Culture, Connection & Creativity – The Andy Way!
Andy Haryana Club blends tradition with trend — a vibrant student community celebrating Haryana’s roots through art, music, and unforgettable moments. From chai meetups to cultural fests, we don’t follow the vibe — we make it.

Our Vibe

Tradition Meets Tomorrow

Vibrant Celebrations

We bring the energy of Haryana's festivals to life. From energetic folk dances and music to celebrating festivals like Lohri together, we are a home away from home, full of life and color.

Community & Connection

Dressed in traditional ghagras and odhnis,this performance was pure energy + tradition. Who says culture can’t be cool? We just proved that a Haryanvi beat can make anyone groove. With those bright skirts, cute smiles, and a sprinkle of sparkle on stage, it was less of a dance and more of a celebration of roots with a remix of vibe.

Modern Creativity

All smiles, folded hands, and pure Haryanvi pride. From the pagdi to the ghagra, every detail screamed Desi elegance with a modern twist.We didn’t just perform we represented a culture, a rhythm, a way of life. Because being rooted doesn’t mean being boring it means knowing where you come from and owning it with style

Student Leadership
