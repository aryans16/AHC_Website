Andy Haryana Club
Celebrating Culture, Connection & Creativity – The Andy Way!
Andy Haryana Club blends tradition with trend — a vibrant student community celebrating Haryana’s roots through art, music, and unforgettable moments. From chai meetups to cultural fests, we don’t follow the vibe — we make it.

Our Vibe

Tradition Meets Tomorrow

Vibrant Celebrations

We bring the energy of Haryana's festivals to life. From energetic folk dances and music to celebrating festivals like Lohri together, we are a home away from home, full of life and color.

Community & Connection

It's not just a club; it's a family. We foster deep connections through 'chai meetups,' collaborative projects, and creating a supportive network for all students on campus.

Modern Creativity

We blend tradition with trend. Our 'Hack & Heritage' events digitize cultural stories, while our talent showcases provide a stage for modern Haryanvi art, music, and poetry.

Student Leadership

This club is run by students, for students. We provide a platform for members to take initiative, lead projects, and gain valuable experience in event management and community building.
